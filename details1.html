Itinerary

Day 1: Toronto - Montreal(540 km)

We'll depart from Toronto early in the morning then traveling to Montreal, stroll around Old Montreal City, and discover the history, heritage, architecture and culture of Montreal. Later we will get a chance to visit the famous Grevin Museum. At night, enjoy the beauty fireworks of Montreal International Fireworks Festival.

Day 2: Lavender Garden - Toronto(540 km)

In the morning, we will visit the Bleu Lavande, the largest in North America, where we can see the different workshops, eat at the bistro, visit the private gardens, and more, all while enjoying beautiful, purple lavender fields. In the evening, we will transfer to Toronto.

  • Montreal, QC

    Montreal, Quebec is the second-largest city in Canada, and the center of French Canadian culture. Called "Canada's Cultural Capital," Montreal offers much to see and do, whether you are looking to explore history or the arts.

    Bleu Lavande

    Bleu Lavande is the largest lavender farm in Canada with over 100,000 true lavender plants. Stroll through the fields of flowering lavender, picnic on site, visit the interpretation centre to learn about the history and farming of lavender.
